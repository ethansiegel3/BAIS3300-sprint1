College Career

I am currently a senior studying Business Analytics and Information Systems at the University of Iowa.

Involvement in AEPi

Throughout my time in college, I was involved in Alpha Epsilon Pi, a Jewish fraternity that emphasizes brotherhood, community service, and professional development. I have held various leadership positions within the fraternity, including Jewish Identity Chair and New Member Educator, and am currently serving as the Alumni Chair.

Iowa Hillel House

As a member of Iowa Hillel, I participated in various events and initiatives aimed at fostering a sense of community and belonging among Jewish students on campus. Through my involvement with Hillel, I was able to connect with other Jewish students, explore my cultural and religious identity, and contribute to the broader campus community. I currently work as a Finance Intern and help manage the organization's budget and financial operations.
